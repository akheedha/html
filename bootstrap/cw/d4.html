Frequently Asked Questions

How can I contact customer support??
You can reach our support team by emailing support@company.com or by calling our helpline at 1800-123-4567. Our team is available Monday to Friday, 9 AM – 6 PM.
What should I do if I forget my account password?
Click on the “Forgot Password” link on the login page, enter your registered email address, and follow the instructions to reset your password. You’ll receive a password reset link via email.
How long does it take to get a response from the support team?
Our support team typically responds within 24 hours on business days. During weekends or holidays, it may take up to 48 hours..
